Allows to specify constraints for asset attributes.

attributeReference : EString - Allows to explicitly create the contraint on a specific attribute of the asset in case this is ambiguous, e.g. a power constraint on the output power of a hybrid heat pump (gas heater power or heat pump power).

Allows to explicitly create the contraint on a specific attribute of the asset in case this is ambiguous, e.g. a power constraint on the output power of a hybrid heat pump (gas heater power or heat pump power). Use the attribute name as a value for attributeReference.
