Composition of different Matters, either mixed (mix of gasses or liquids) or layered.

Examples of layered Matters - a construction of a wall, roof, or floor with isolation - double or triple glazing consisting of multiple layers - a heatnetwork pipe or electrical cable consisting of multiple layers
